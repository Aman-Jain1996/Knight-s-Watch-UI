Badge

Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component. Badge can be used to display numbers, online / offline status, depending on where they are used.

Badges used with Avatar

They can be used to show the availability status of a user. We will be using our 'avatar' classes here to make avatar.

Class-Usage : online | sleep | dnd 

                        
            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar" src="image-source" alt="Badge">
                <div class="online"></div>
            </div>


            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar sm" src="image-source" alt="Badge">
                <div class="sleep"></div>
            </div>


            <div class="badge">
                <img class="avatar square lg" src="image-source" alt="Badge">
                <div class="dnd"></div>
            </div>

Badges with Icons

Badges can also be used to show count of something when clubbed with any icon e.g; Count of items in cart , count of mails etc.

Class-Usage : numbered | numbered-success | numbered-danger

4
10
99+
                        
            <div class="badge">
                <ion-icon name="cart-outline"></ion-icon>
                <div class="numbered">4</div>
            </div>


            <div class="badge">
                <ion-icon name="mail-outline"></ion-icon>
                <div class="numbered-success">10</div>
            </div>


            <div class="badge">
                <ion-icon name="calendar-outline"></ion-icon>
                <div class="numbered-danger">99+</div>
            </div>
