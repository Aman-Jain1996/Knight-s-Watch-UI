Images

Images are the main ingredient of any website that looks visually appealing and can provide the visual information related to anything. Images in this component library expand upto the width of the container they are in.

Square Image

Square Images are images with equal height and width and by default they have a dimension of 150px X 150px.

Class-Usage : img-square 

                        
                <img class="img-square" src="image-source" alt="man riding a cycle" >
                            
                <img class="img-square" src="image-source" alt="car image" >

Rounded Image

Rounded Images have rounded bordres and they have dimension of 150px X 150px.

Class-Usage : img-circle 

                        
                <img class="img-circle" src="image-source" alt="man riding a cycle">

                <img class="img-circle" src="image-source" alt="car image" >

Responsive Image

Responsive Images will take the width and height of its parent container and they also respond if srceen-size is changed.
Note : Resize the browser window, to see responsive image work.

Class-Usage : img-responsive 

                        
            <div>
                <img class="img-responsive" src="image-source" alt="man riding a cycle">
            </div>
