Cards

Cards provide an easy and modular way to show information related to anything with some images or some action buttons.

Simple Card

This type of card usually contains text related to some product with a little description.

Class-Usage : card

Batman

Batman is a superhero who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger. 

                        
            <div class="card">
                <p class="card-heading">
                    Batman
                </p>

                <p class="card-description">
                    Batman is a superhero who appears in American comic 
                    books published by DC Comics. The character was 
                    created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger.
                </p>

            </div>
