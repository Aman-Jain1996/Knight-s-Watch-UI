Modal

Modals are used to show interactive dialogs and notifications to your website users on top of everything present on screen.

Default Modal

This type of modal usually contains some information with some action buttons.

Class-Usage : modal

Lorem, ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit.

Cancel
Confirm
                        
                        <div class="modal">

                            <h4 class="modal-heading">Lorem, ipsum dolor sit amet 
                            consectetur adipisicing elit.
                            </h4>

                            <div class="modal-action-container">
                                <div class="cancel">Cancel</div>
                                <div class="confirm">Confirm</div>
                            </div>

                        </div>
