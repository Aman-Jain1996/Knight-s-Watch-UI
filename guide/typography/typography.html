Typography

Knight's Watch is using 'Josefin Sans' font provided by Google Fonts internally for font-styling throught the Project.

Headings

Headings are use to give the context of the information wriiten below it.

Variant-Usage : heading-xl (36px) | -lg (24px) | -m (14px) | -sm (10px)

small

medium

large

extra large 

                        
                                <p class="heading-sm">
                                    I am a small heading
                                </p>

                                <p class="heading-m">
                                    I am a medium heading
                                </p>

                                <p class="heading-lg">
                                    I am a large heading
                                </p>

                                <p class="heading-xl">
                                    I am a extra large heading
                                </p>
